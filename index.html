After earning an automotive engineering degree, I immersed myself in customer experience ​leadership roles at Xero and Mobilize Financial Services, building people and project management ​skills. But deep down, I yearned for a more creative outlet so in January 2023, I embarked on a coding ​renaissance.

This journey of mine has been fuelled by a customer-centric mindset and a thirst for creativity. I ​embrace challenges, learn from each endeavour, and I’m striving to make a positive impact in the ​world of software engineering. The path ahead is full of endless possibilities, and I can't wait to ​discover what lies beyond the next line of code.
